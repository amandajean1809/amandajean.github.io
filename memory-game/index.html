Match the Owls

Owls are members of the magnificent raptor family. Many people think they all look alike. This game will check to see how observant you are in telling them apart!!

Instructions

Once the players enter their names, the player that starts is picked randomly. That player will start selecting the cards on the screen in pairs. If the images on the back of the cards match, a point is earned and the player's turn continues as long as the pair selections match. At that point the second player will begin picking card pairs until a mis-match occurrs. Once the cards are all turned over the player with the most matches wins the game.
